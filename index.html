Scholarship Finder Mobile App
A new mobile application that solves a real world issue for potential users.
UX/UI Design Project
Project Overview
The objective of this project was to make the scholarship search and application process more efficient and effective for applicants. We aimed to provide users with a less daunting and tedious experience by offering filtering, tracking, and archiving, as well as educational resources, all on one convenient platform. Our mission was to create an app that improved the accessibility of scholarships to those in need of financial aid.

User Research
In order to gauge how individuals felt about the current scholarship process and in an attempt to identify any areas of improvement, our group conducted five user interviews and sent out an online survey. We chose participants who were current students or recent graduates who had experience applying for scholarships in the past three years to ensure that our findings reflected the present state of the scholarship process.  
We first constructed a user research plan and interview script to guide our interviews and further research. Our goal was to uncover any user frustrations and identify the path users were taking to find and apply for scholarships.
We targeted high school, college, recent graduates, and post-grad students as our ideal interviewees in an attempt to get the most accurate depiction of the current scholarship climate.
Our team conducted five user interviews and sent out a survey hoping to gain as much insight on the scholarship process as possible. We would use these valuable insights to begin brainstorming improved solutions.
We synthesized our interview data into an affinity diagram, empathy map, and user persona and were able to confidently conclude that applicants are overwhelmed by the lack of structure in the current scholarship search and that there was an opportunity to design a more efficient alternative. Many applicants spend countless hours searching for scholarships, which can become extremely frustrating and discouraging. The lack of organization and visibility within the vast landscape of scholarships hinders their access to future life-changing opportunities.

Ideation
Our team began brainstorming features that would alleviate user frustrations and pain points and maximize the app’s potential. We condensed our ideas with a feature prioritization and value proposition, extracting the most practical and beneficial solutions. We decided we wanted Scholarship Finder to assist applicants with filtering and tracking scholarships, as well as archiving their in-progress applications in one convenient location. It would consolidate applicants’ needs and make their scholarship journey as efficient and effective as possible.
Feature Prioritization
We first and foremost wanted to prioritize filtering, which would allow users to search for scholarships based on unique case specific parameters.
Bookmarking or archiving would allow users to save any applications that they were interested in, as well as the progress of any incomplete applications.
Lastly, we felt it was important to incorporate a learn tab filled with resources on the scholarship process, so our users would have the opportunity to educate themselves and make more informed decisions.
Integrating tracking into our application was essential to extricating the user’s need for secondary platforms, enhancing our apps efficiency.
Storyboard
Jacob has applied to a few schools and is now looking for scholarships to help him cover some of his tuition.
He began his search very optimistic, but after hours of sifting through scholarships, he’s beginning to wonder if he’ll ever find any that are relevant to him.
Weeks of searching with no luck have left him discouraged and unsure how he will be able to attend a university without financial help.
One day, Jacob stumbles upon a new app called Scholarship Finder that is said to help applicants filter, track, and archive their scholarship applications. He decides to give it a chance.
He filters through scholarships based on his demographics and career path and fills out as many applications as he can. He also tracks the status of his applications in app and uses the learn tab to improve his odds.
After a lot of hard work and dedication, Jacob is awarded several scholarships that will help him pay for his coming tuition and books. Thanks to Scholarship Finder, he is one step closer to achieving his dreams.
